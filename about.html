About us

NESW Logistics is a 100% neutral freight consolidation agent providing line-haul services to main centers in South Africa.

Our mission is to partner with the Courier & Logistics industry to give them a cost effective and reliable long-distance solution to enable them to focus on their last mile.

But how do we achive this?

  • Through variable space allocation, we give buying power back to the customer allowing them to save on cost by only paying for what they use.
  • Through direct network management, customers know NESW is handeling their cargo directly thus reducing the possibility of handeling errors, misroutes or delayes.
